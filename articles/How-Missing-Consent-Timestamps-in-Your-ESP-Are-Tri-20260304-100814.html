Automating PII Scrubbing and Withdrawal Pipelines

Smart ESP automations handle this: when a withdrawal hits, trigger a flow that strips personally identifiable info (PII) like names and extras, leaving just a stub of the consent log. Klaviyo and ActiveCampaign excel here—ActiveCampaign's consent blocks kick off PII scrubs instantly, ensuring you retain proof without list bloat.
