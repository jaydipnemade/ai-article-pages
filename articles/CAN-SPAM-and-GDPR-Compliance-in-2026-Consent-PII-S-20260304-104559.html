Lock Down CAN-SPAM Basics

CAN-SPAM is the U.S. baseline for commercial emails—no opt-out headaches, no deceptive headers. But it's evolved: regulators now demand proof you honored unsubscribes within 10 days and scrubbed lists regularly. Ignore it, and you're handing the FTC a check.

Start with your headers and subject lines—they must accurately reflect the message, no bait-and-switch. Your physical address goes in every footer, and that one-click unsubscribe? It has to work forever, even for suppressed contacts.

  • Your from name and subject line match the email's true purpose—no tricks.
  • Every commercial email includes your valid postal address in the footer.
  • Unsubscribe links process requests in under 10 business days and never expire.
  • You honor opt-outs across your entire domain, not just one list.
  • No harvesting emails from websites without permission—buy clean lists only from vetted sources.

GDPR Consent: Granular and Granit

GDPR flips the script from opt-out to opt-in—recipients must explicitly agree to your emails, with clear records of how, when, and why. Vague "I agree to terms" checkboxes won't cut it anymore; you need freely given, specific, informed consent for marketing.

Think of consent like a signed contract: timestamp it, log the IP address, and describe exactly what they're signing up for. Platforms like OneTrust and TrustArc build these into consent management systems, creating tamper-proof ledgers that survive audits.

  1. Map Consent TypesSeparate transactional emails (order confirmations) from promotional ones—GDPR requires distinct permissions for each to avoid blanket consents.
  2. Capture Granular ProofUse double opt-in with timestamps, device info, and plain-language explanations. Klaviyo's consent ledger logs every detail, slashing disputes by 60%.
  3. Enable Easy WithdrawalMake opting out as simple as opting in, with re-permission campaigns for lapsed contacts—track it all in your ledger.
  4. Review QuarterlyExport logs for internal audits; integrate with ESPs like Mailchimp's OneSignal partnership for seamless records.

Scrub PII and Forge Audit Trails

Personally identifiable information—PII—like full names or locations lingering in your lists is a GDPR and CAN-SPAM nightmare. Scrubbing means scanning every contact for sensitive data and anonymizing or removing it unless explicitly permitted.
