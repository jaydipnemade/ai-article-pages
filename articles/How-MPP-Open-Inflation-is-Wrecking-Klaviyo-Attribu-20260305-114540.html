MPP's Hidden Attribution Killer

Apple's MPP kicks in for about 78% of Apple Mail users on iOS 15 and later. When an email arrives, their system automatically reaches out to load images and tracking pixels from your server, mimicking an open before the recipient even glances at it. This creates a flood of false opens, bumping average open rates across benchmarks by a solid 25%, based on Litmus's analysis of over a billion emails in early 2024.

Here's the real damage: in multi-touch attribution setups common in Klaviyo, these phantom opens trick models into overcrediting email. Think first-touch or linear models—they see "opens" everywhere and assign email way more influence on the path to purchase. Forrester's latest trends peg this overcredit at 20-30%, meaning your reported revenue per email is puffed up, leaving you blind to what's actually driving sales.
